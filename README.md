### Building ethical driving through awareness  

DriveAware is a structured awareness-based ethical driving training platform designed to build long-term responsible driving habits. Instead of focusing on punishment or enforcement, DriveAware promotes behavioral growth through progressive scenario-based challenges, real-time feedback, and positive reinforcement.

---

## ğŸŒ Problem Statement

Traffic rules exist everywhere, yet accidents continue to rise. The core issue is not the lack of rules but the lack of internalized ethical driving habits. Most drivers act responsibly only when enforcement is present. This temporary compliance does not create lasting behavior change.

DriveAware bridges this gap by transforming ethical driving awareness into a structured, progressive learning experience.

---

## ğŸ’¡ Our Solution

DriveAware provides:

- 50 progressive awareness-based chapters  
- 10 real-life scenario decisions per chapter  
- 60-second timed ethical decision challenges  
- Structured growth from basic awareness to advanced ethical thinking  
- Real-time feedback instead of punishment  
- Personal progress tracking  
- 20 achievement badges for milestones  
- Advanced analytics dashboard with visual insights  

The platform focuses on awareness, reflection, and improvement rather than competition or ranking.

---

## ğŸ§  How It Works

1. User registers and starts their awareness journey.
2. Chapters unlock sequentially after completion.
3. Each chapter contains 10 real-life driving scenarios.
4. Each scenario must be answered within 60 seconds.
5. Feedback is provided immediately with insight on impact.
6. Scores and behavior patterns are tracked.
7. Progress analytics help users understand growth areas.
8. Badges unlock based on milestones and achievements.

DriveAware builds habits through exposure, repetition, and structured reflection.
